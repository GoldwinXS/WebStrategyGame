HOW TO PLAY

⚓ COMBAT

  • Click / Tap ship — Select it
  • Click / Tap ocean — Move selected ship
  • Click / Tap enemy — Set as attack target
  • SPACE or ⏸ button — Tactical pause
  • Q / ▲ button — Ascend (shallower)
  • E / ▼ button — Descend (deeper)
  • Ships auto-fire at their assigned target
  • Scroll / pinch to zoom · middle-drag to pan

🗺 CAMPAIGN

  • Navigate a node map across 3 sectors
  • ⚔ Combat — defeat all enemies to win
  • ☠ Elite — powerful enemies, greater loot
  • ? Event — story choices with consequences
  • $ Supply — spend credits on upgrades
  • ✚ Rest — repair your fleet
  • ★ Boss — clear each sector boss to advance

⚡ FLEET

  • Flagship Heraklion is irreplaceable — protect it
  • Other ships destroyed in combat are lost forever
  • Earn credits to buy ships and upgrades
  • Ships that survive gain experience and level up
  • Upgrades are applied between encounters

👾 FACTIONS

  • Keth'vari — Organic bio-ships, fast and swarming
  • Shard Collective — Crystalline, heavily armored
  • Leviathans — Ancient creatures, titanic scale
